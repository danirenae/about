About

I never thought I could be a developer. It seemed like this intangible, magical thing that someone like me simply could not do. This mentality stood in my way for a long time.

I have always been interested in technology. I studied Communications and Media at Bellarmine University in Louisville, KY, were I hoped to sort of "hang" in the technology space a bit. I dabbled in film and digital photography, tried be do a bit of film-making and editing with FinalCutPro and continued to write about the things I loved, while keeping those things hidden in my journal for my eyes only.

Two years ago I decided to give some online coding courses a try and I fell in love with software development. Shortly thereafter, I applied to Nashville Software School where I committed my evenings and weekends to coding, without giving up my day job. In January, I decided to go all in. I quit my full-time job and dove head first into my courses and my own endeavors with technology.
